The Badlander Complex

About

The Badlander complex is a family of bars and entertainment venues located in historic downtown Missoula Montana. The Badlander provides a unique nightlife experience with something to appeal to everyone. We have five bars that are all connected, all offering a different experience. The complex includes a casino, a nightclub with live music, a pool hall, a family friendly restaurant, and a liquor store. Come visit us and experience it for yourself!
